How it Works?

Laundr-easy is a product designed to significantly reduce the time it takes to do your laundry. The user would simply mount the clothes on a platform and an electrically powered mechanism would quickly fold them. In addition, This platform can hold multiple clothes at once for increased efficiency. The end result would be an organized, neatly folded stack of clothes.
